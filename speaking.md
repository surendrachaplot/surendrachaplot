I talk about stuff I've spent way too many years thinking about.

## What I'm Happy to Talk About

**Building product at scale** — How we grew Wise from 6M to 15.5M customers. What broke. What I'd do differently.

**Cross-border payments and fintech** — Moving money globally. Regulatory mess. Why this space still has so much opportunity.

**Leading product teams** — Managing large PM teams across countries. Some things work, some don't. I've made plenty of mistakes.

**Angel investing** — From a founder's perspective: what I look for, how I think about it, why I'm putting 20% of my time into UK startups.

**Building in different markets** — I've operated across India, Southeast Asia, Europe. Cultural context matters more than people think.

## Some Things I've Done

**Hong Kong Fintech Festival 2023** — Main stage. Still can't quite believe they let me up there. Talked about the future of cross-border payments in APAC.

**25+ publications and podcasts** — Everything from TechCrunch to niche fintech newsletters. I'll do most formats if I think the audience will actually get something out of it.

## What I Don't Do

- "Thought leadership" fluff. If you want buzzwords, I'm not your person.
- Panels where I'm supposed to nod along. I'd rather not be there.
- Sales pitches in disguise. Life's too short.

## How to Reach Me

If you're organising an event and think I might be a good fit, send me a note.

Tell me:
- What the event is
- Who the audience is (be specific — "tech people" doesn't help)
- What you'd like me to talk about
- Why you think it's a good match

I'll say yes if I think I can help. I'll say no if I don't. Simple as that.
