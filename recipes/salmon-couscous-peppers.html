Salmon with Couscous, Roasted Peppers, and Herbs

25 min · Serves 2

Roasted peppers and couscous do the quiet support work here. The salmon still gets top billing, as it should.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 120g wholewheat couscous
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow pepper, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zest and half the juice
  • Small handful parsley, chopped
  • Small handful mint, chopped
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 220C. Toss the peppers and onion with half the olive oil and roast for 15 minutes.
  2. Rub the salmon with the rest of the oil, the smoked paprika, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Add to the tray for the last 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Cover the couscous with boiling water according to the pack instructions and leave for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  4. Mix the couscous with lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, mint, and the roasted peppers and onion.
  5. Serve the salmon over the couscous and vegetables.
