Tuna, Cannellini Bean & Caper Pasta

20 min · Serves 2

The kind of pantry dinner that feels suspiciously competent. Use wholewheat pasta if you want to push fibre a bit harder.

Ingredients

  • 160g wholewheat spaghetti or linguine
  • 1 x 400g tin cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tin tuna in olive oil, drained lightly
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • Small handful parsley, chopped
  • Black pepper

Method

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente, reserving a mug of pasta water.
  2. Warm the olive oil in a large pan. Add the garlic, chilli flakes, and capers for 1 minute.
  3. Add the beans and a splash of pasta water. Stir for 2 minutes until warmed through.
  4. Add the tuna, lemon zest, and most of the lemon juice. Break the tuna into chunks.
  5. Toss in the drained pasta with more pasta water as needed to loosen. Finish with parsley, black pepper, and the remaining lemon juice.
