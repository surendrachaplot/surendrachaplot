<article class="page recipe-detail">
  <h1>Cod Traybake with Tomatoes, Olives & Chickpeas</h1>
  <div class="recipe-meta"><span>Cook: 30 min</span><span>Serves: 2</span><span>Heart-healthy</span></div>

  <p>A traybake for when you want something Mediterranean and low-effort. Chickpeas stop the cod from being lonely and insubstantial.</p>

  <h2>Ingredients</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>2 cod fillets</li>
    <li>1 x 400g tin chickpeas, drained and rinsed</li>
    <li>250g cherry tomatoes</li>
    <li>12 olives, roughly torn</li>
    <li>2 garlic cloves, sliced</li>
    <li>1/2 tsp dried oregano</li>
    <li>1/2 tsp chilli flakes</li>
    <li>2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil</li>
    <li>1 lemon</li>
    <li>Salt and black pepper</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Method</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Heat the oven to 220C.</li>
    <li>Toss the chickpeas, tomatoes, olives, garlic, oregano, chilli flakes, and 1 tablespoon olive oil in a baking dish. Roast for 15 minutes.</li>
    <li>Nestle the cod into the dish, season, and drizzle with the remaining olive oil.</li>
    <li>Roast for another 10 to 12 minutes until the fish flakes easily and the tomatoes have collapsed.</li>
    <li>Finish with lemon juice and serve straight from the dish.</li>
  </ol>
