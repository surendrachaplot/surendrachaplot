Salmon Fishcakes with Mustard-Herb Slaw

30 min · Serves 2

Fishcakes can go badly wrong if they turn into a breadcrumb delivery system. These stay mostly salmon, potato, herbs, and a sharp mustard slaw on the side.

Ingredients

  • 250g salmon fillet
  • 250g baby potatoes
  • 1 spring onion, finely sliced
  • Small handful parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1/4 small cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 small carrot, grated
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Small handful dill, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes for 12 to 15 minutes until tender. Drain and mash roughly.
  2. While they cook, roast or pan-cook the salmon until just done, then flake into a bowl.
  3. Mix the salmon with the mashed potato, spring onion, herbs, mustard, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 fishcakes.
  4. Pan-fry the fishcakes in the olive oil for 3 to 4 minutes on each side until golden.
  5. Toss the cabbage and carrot with the wholegrain mustard, olive oil, lemon juice, dill, salt, and pepper. Serve alongside the fishcakes.
