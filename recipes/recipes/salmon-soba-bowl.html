Salmon Soba Bowl with Edamame and Sesame Greens

25 min · Serves 2

This is the dependable salmon bowl: oily fish, greens, edamame, buckwheat noodles, and enough sesame to keep it interesting without wrecking the brief.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 120g soba noodles
  • 150g shelled edamame
  • 2 large handfuls spinach or bok choy
  • 1 tbsp low-salt soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp white miso
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Method

  1. Mix the soy sauce, miso, honey, and ginger. Rub over the salmon.
  2. Roast or air-fry the salmon at 220C for 10 to 12 minutes until just cooked.
  3. Cook the soba noodles. In the last 2 minutes, add the edamame. Drain and toss with the sesame oil.
  4. Wilt the greens in the hot noodle pan or quickly in a skillet with a splash of water.
  5. Divide the noodles, edamame, and greens between bowls. Top with salmon, sesame seeds, and lime wedges.
