Mackerel Rice Bowl with Cucumber, Herbs, and Soy-Lime Dressing

20 min · Serves 2

Mackerel is doing the heavy lifting here. The rest is just making sure it lands with crunch, acid, and enough rice to feel like dinner.

Ingredients

  • 150g brown rice
  • 2 smoked mackerel fillets, flaked
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced into half-moons
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • Small handful mint, chopped
  • Small handful coriander, chopped
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 tbsp low-salt soy sauce
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Cook the brown rice according to the pack instructions.
  2. Whisk together the lime juice, soy sauce, sesame oil, and honey.
  3. Toss the cucumber and spring onion with half the dressing.
  4. Divide the hot rice between bowls. Top with flaked mackerel, dressed cucumber, mint, and coriander.
  5. Spoon over the remaining dressing and finish with sesame seeds.
