Cod Traybake with Tomatoes, Olives, and Chickpeas

30 min · Serves 2

A traybake for when you want something Mediterranean and low-effort. Chickpeas stop the cod from being lonely and insubstantial.

Ingredients

  • 2 cod fillets
  • 1 x 400g tin chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 12 olives, roughly torn
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 220C.
  2. Toss the chickpeas, tomatoes, olives, garlic, oregano, chilli flakes, and 1 tablespoon olive oil in a baking dish. Roast for 15 minutes.
  3. Nestle the cod into the dish, season, and drizzle with the remaining olive oil.
  4. Roast for another 10 to 12 minutes until the fish flakes easily and the tomatoes have collapsed.
  5. Finish with lemon juice and serve straight from the dish.
