Sardine, White Bean & Fennel Lemon Salad

15 min · Serves 2

Tinned sardines are one of the cheapest ways to get real omega-3s into dinner. White beans do the fibre work, fennel keeps it bright, and lemon stops the whole thing tasting worthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 fennel bulb, very thinly sliced
  • 1 x 400g tin cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tins sardines in olive oil, drained lightly
  • 1 small shallot, finely sliced
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Small handful parsley or dill, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Put the fennel in a bowl with half the lemon juice and a pinch of salt. Toss and leave it for 5 minutes to soften slightly.
  2. Whisk the remaining lemon juice with the zest, olive oil, mustard, and black pepper.
  3. Add the beans, shallot, and herbs to the fennel. Toss with the dressing.
  4. Gently fold through the sardines in large pieces so they keep some shape.
  5. Taste and adjust with more lemon, salt, or pepper. Eat as is or with a slice of sourdough.
