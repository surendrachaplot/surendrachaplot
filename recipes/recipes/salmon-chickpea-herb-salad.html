Salmon, Chickpea & Herb Salad with Lemon-Caper Dressing

20 min · Serves 2

This is what happens when salmon meets a pantry that knows what it’s doing. Chickpeas bring fibre, herbs keep it bright, and the capers stop it drifting into health-food boredom.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 x 400g tin chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 little gem lettuce, leaves separated
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced
  • Small handful parsley, chopped
  • Small handful dill, chopped
  • 1 tbsp capers, roughly chopped
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Roast or air-fry the salmon at 220C for 10 to 12 minutes until just cooked.
  2. Meanwhile whisk the lemon zest and juice with the olive oil, mustard, capers, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Toss the chickpeas, lettuce, cucumber, parsley, and dill with most of the dressing.
  4. Break the salmon into large pieces and lay over the salad.
  5. Spoon over the remaining dressing and eat while the salmon is still warm.
