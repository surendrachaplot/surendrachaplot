Miso Mackerel Rice Bowl with Broccoli and Cucumber

20 min · Serves 2

Mackerel is the better move here. Richer than sardines, still cheap, and it takes a miso dressing without turning the bowl into a tin of regret.

Ingredients

  • 150g brown rice
  • 2 tins mackerel in olive oil, drained lightly
  • 1 small head broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp white or brown miso
  • 1 tbsp low-salt soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Cook the brown rice according to the pack instructions.
  2. Steam or boil the broccoli for 3 to 4 minutes until just tender.
  3. Whisk together the miso, soy sauce, lime juice, honey, sesame oil, and 1 tbsp hot water.
  4. Toss the cucumber and spring onions with a spoonful of the dressing.
  5. Divide the rice between bowls. Top with broccoli, mackerel, and dressed cucumber, then spoon over the rest of the dressing and finish with sesame seeds.
