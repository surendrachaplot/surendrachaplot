  <h1>Mackerel Rice Bowl with Cucumber, Herbs & Soy-Lime Dressing</h1>
  <div class="recipe-meta"><span>Cook: 20 min</span><span>Serves: 2</span><span>Heart-healthy</span></div>

  <p>Mackerel is doing the heavy lifting here. The rest is just making sure it lands with crunch, acid, and enough rice to feel like dinner.</p>

  <h2>Ingredients</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>150g brown rice</li>
    <li>2 smoked mackerel fillets, flaked</li>
    <li>1/2 cucumber, sliced into half-moons</li>
    <li>2 spring onions, finely sliced</li>
    <li>Small handful mint, chopped</li>
    <li>Small handful coriander, chopped</li>
    <li>1 lime, juice only</li>
    <li>1 tbsp low-salt soy sauce</li>
    <li>1 tsp toasted sesame oil</li>
    <li>1 tsp honey</li>
    <li>1 tsp sesame seeds</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Method</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Cook the brown rice according to the pack instructions.</li>
    <li>Meanwhile, whisk together the lime juice, soy sauce, sesame oil, and honey.</li>
    <li>Toss the cucumber and spring onion with half the dressing.</li>
    <li>Divide the hot rice between bowls. Top with flaked mackerel, dressed cucumber, mint, and coriander.</li>
    <li>Spoon over the remaining dressing and finish with sesame seeds.</li>
  </ol>
