Mackerel Udon Bowl with Ginger Greens

20 min · Serves 2

Smoked mackerel plus quick noodles is borderline unfair. Ginger, greens, and lime stop it feeling heavy, which is the whole point.

Ingredients

  • 2 smoked mackerel fillets, flaked
  • 2 portions udon noodles
  • 2 large handfuls spinach or pak choi
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp low-salt soy sauce
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Cook the udon according to the pack instructions.
  2. Whisk together the ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, and lime juice.
  3. Wilt the greens in a large pan with a splash of water for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Add the drained noodles, spring onions, and half the dressing to the greens and toss.
  5. Divide between bowls, top with flaked mackerel, spoon over the rest of the dressing, and finish with sesame seeds.
