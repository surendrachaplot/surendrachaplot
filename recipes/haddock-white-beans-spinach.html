Haddock with White Beans, Spinach, and Lemon

20 min · Serves 2

A one-pan white fish dinner with enough beans and greens to make it count. Lemon and garlic do the rescue work that butter usually gets hired for.

Ingredients

  • 2 haddock fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • 1 x 400g tin cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 150g spinach
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes, optional
  • Small handful parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Season the haddock with salt and pepper.
  2. Warm half the olive oil in a wide pan and cook the haddock for 3 to 4 minutes per side, depending on thickness, until just done. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Add the remaining oil, garlic, and chilli flakes to the pan. Cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the beans, spinach, lemon zest, and a splash of water. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes until the spinach wilts and the beans are hot.
  5. Stir through the lemon juice and parsley, then sit the haddock back on top and serve.
