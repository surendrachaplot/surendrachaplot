## Recent Favourites

Books that changed how I think:

**On Work & Building**

*The Hard Thing About Hard Things* by Ben Horowitz — Best book on what it's actually like to run a company. No sugarcoating.

*Zero to One* by Peter Thiel — Framework on contrarian thinking. Good stuff.

**On Life & Curiosity**

*Kitchen Confidential* by Anthony Bourdain — It's not about cooking. It's about doing something with your life and giving a damn. This book is why I write the way I do.

*Into the Wild* by Jon Krakauer — Haunted me for weeks after I finished it. Still does.

**On Money & Business**

*Barbarians at the Gate* — Story of RJR Nabisco. Reads like fiction. Great lesson on ego and what happens when people stop caring about the actual business.

*Poor Charlie's Almanack* — Charlie Munger's mental models. Dense, but worth it.
