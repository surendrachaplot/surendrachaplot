Jack of a few things. Trying to master some of them.

Made a really silly feature film called The Bet for Bollywood in 2009-ish. We were a bunch of over-excited and over-ambitious teenagers. The film was rubbish. Those days of making the film though were amazing.

Co-founded a magazine called [21Fools](https://21fools.com/) which then became an online greeting cards company. Now they make thoughtful, wonderful, sustainable products. All credit there to [this guy](https://www.instagram.com/divyanshuasopa).

Spent the last decade or so learning the world of Internet.

Lived in Mumbai, Delhi, Jakarta and now in Singapore. Still consider the beautiful City of Lakes — [Udaipur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Udaipur) my home 'home'.

Until recently, I was Head of Global Product at [Wise](https://wise.com/). We grew from 6 million customers to 15.5 million, revenue from £420 million to £1.3 billion. The numbers sound impressive, but what actually matters: more people can send money home without getting ripped off.

I'm also an angel investor. I back founders building things that matter — companies like [Cosmos](https://cosmos.video) and [Thinking Machines Lab](https://thinkingmachines.ai). Moving to London in April 2026.

When I'm not working, I take photos ([@chaplot](https://instagram.com/chaplot)), read ([goodreads.com/chaplot](https://goodreads.com/chaplot)), or travel somewhere new. Fan of Anthony Bourdain — liked that he was honest, curious, gave a damn about craft.
