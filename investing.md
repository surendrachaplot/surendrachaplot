I invest in founders who are building things that matter.

I know what it's like to start something with nothing but conviction and way too much work. Been there. Figured I should help other people do it too.

## What I Invest In

**Fintech** — It's what I know. 17 years in payments, moving money across borders, marketplaces. If you're fixing something broken in how money moves — especially for people who get screwed — I'm interested.

**AI** — I've backed companies like [Cosmos](https://cosmos.video) and [Thinking Machines Lab](https://thinkingmachines.ai). AI done right can actually help people. That's the part I care about.

**UK & US Startups** — I'm focused on the UK right now. Putting 20% of my time and money here because I think something interesting is happening with UK founders. But I'll look anywhere if the team and problem are right.

## What I Look For

Three things:

1. **The founder** — Are you the kind of person who won't quit? Can you make decisions with 60% of the information? Do people want to work with you?

2. **The problem** — Is it real? Are people actually in pain? Or is this a "nice to have" pretending to be a must-have?

3. **The why** — Why you? What do you know that nobody else does?

I write smaller checks early on, sometimes follow-on later if things are working. What I really offer is time — product thinking, operational experience, an honest perspective from someone who's scaled products to millions of users.

## How to Reach Out

Send me an email. Keep it short.

Tell me:
- What you're building (2–3 sentences)
- The problem you're solving
- Why you're the right team
- What you're looking for

No pitch decks first. No 10-page attachments. Just talk to me like a human.

If I don't reply, don't take it personally. I read everything, but I only reply to things I think I can help with.

Let's build something.
